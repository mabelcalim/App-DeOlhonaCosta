This app aims to engage the citizen scientist to create a database about your perception and identification of biodiversity in coastal regions.

To do so:
* This app requires a username and password to access the database just to identify the user about their history of collections in citizen science project.
* This app accesses the camera to capture and identify biodiversity in coastal regions.
* This information is collect in a database only for academic aims.

No personal information is collected by the app, or transmitted in any other way.
